I’ currently a Dunham Jackson Assistant Professor at University of Minnesota.

I finished my PhD in mathematics at Uppsala University in June 2024 under the supervision of Jordi-Lluís Figueras (Uppsala University), Javier Gómez Serrano (Brown University) and Warwick Tucker (Monash University).

My research interests include fluid mechanics, spectral geometry, computer algebra and computer assisted proofs.
